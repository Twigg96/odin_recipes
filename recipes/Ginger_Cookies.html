Ginger Cookies

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¾ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup butter or margarine, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • ¼ cup molasses
  • 1 tablespoon water
